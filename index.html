ResQNow Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [April 2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to ResQNow! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Your name, email, phone number, and emergency contact details (name and phone number) when you register.
  • Location Data: Real-time location data for emergency services and safety monitoring.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, crash reports, and feature usage statistics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the ResQNow app.
  • Notify emergency contacts when necessary.
  • Detect and alert users about potential dangers.
  • Ensure user authentication and account security.

4. How We Share Your Information

  • Emergency Contacts: If you activate an alert, your emergency contacts will have access to your location.
  • Law Enforcement: If required by law or in emergencies to ensure user safety.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate the app, such as cloud storage and analytics providers.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Update your personal information within the app settings.
  • Delete your account, which will remove all personal information and revoke access to all services.
  • Revoke emergency contact access at any time.
  • Disable location tracking (though this may limit app functionality).
  • Delete location-related data once location services are stopped.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as necessary to provide services. If you delete your account, we remove all associated data, except where required by law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
